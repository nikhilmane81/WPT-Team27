About us

Sneak-A-Look is an Indian company owned by PNS Industries. Welcome to Thrifter, your number one source for all things hype. We're dedicated to providing you the very best of sneakers, with an emphasis on authenticity, pricing, and the best experience for getting your Ws.

Founded in 2022, Sneak-A-Look has come a long way from its beginnings in CDAC Mumbai. When we first started out, our passion for sneakers and eco-friendly products to fight fast fashion drove them to start their own business.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
